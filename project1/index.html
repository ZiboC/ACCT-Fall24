Project 1: Kludging as a Creative Approach to Technology

Project Overview

In this project, I used ml5.js and p5.js to create a hand pose drawing interface. Users can easily draw in front of the camera using hand gestures, with the index finger acting as the brush. The camera continuously tracks hand movements, and the brush color changes when all fingers are spread. Additionally, I incorporated a projector, rear projection film, and glass to achieve a holographic effect.
